How the match works
  1. Contacts are matched between the two files by name.
  2. Matched contacts: Current / <1 Month / 1 Month / 2 Months / Older columns are overwritten from the source. The Total column keeps its formula.
  3. If a contact's new Total is negative, every column for that row is set to zero.
  4. Contacts only in the source are added as new rows at the bottom.
  5. Contacts only in the working sheet are left exactly as they were.
  6. No other rows, columns, or sheets are changed.
